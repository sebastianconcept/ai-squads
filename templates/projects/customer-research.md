### JTBD Interview Framework
**Structured approach for job-focused interviews**

#### Interview Structure
1. **Job Discovery** (15 minutes)
   - Understand participant's role and context
   - Identify recent job completion experiences
   - Explore job triggers and circumstances

2. **Job Mapping** (20 minutes)
   - Map job progress step by step
   - Identify pain points and satisfaction gaps
   - Explore emotional and social dimensions

3. **Solution Exploration** (15 minutes)
   - Discuss current solutions and alternatives
   - Identify what works and what doesn't
   - Explore solution preferences and priorities

4. **Validation** (10 minutes)
   - Validate understanding of jobs and gaps
   - Confirm solution alignment with jobs
   - Identify additional research needs

#### Key Interview Questions

**Job Discovery Questions**
- "Tell me about a recent time when you needed to [JOB CONTEXT]"
- "What were you trying to accomplish?"
- "What triggered this need?"
- "What was the context or situation?"

**Job Progress Questions**
- "Walk me through what you did step by step"
- "Where did you struggle or get frustrated?"
- "What worked well? What didn't?"
- "How did you know you were making progress?"

**Satisfaction Questions**
- "How satisfied were you with the outcome?"
- "What would have made this experience better?"
- "What alternatives did you consider?"
- "What would you do differently next time?"
