## Overview
The `sulaco` squad is a squad of agents collaborating intelligently to plan and execute tasks in assigned projects. The projects plan, mission, specs and tasks are created and found in `.ai-squads/projects/[PROJECT_NAME]` workspace. It serves as a smart context guiding system for all the agents in the squad feel oriented on what to do next.

## Core Purpose
- **Micro SaaS projects**: Efficiently plan and execute micro SaaS projects with focus on:
  - Customer validation and product-market fit
  - Subscription business models and pricing optimization
  - Growth marketing and customer acquisition
  - Customer success and retention strategies
  - Scalable infrastructure and performance optimization

## Micro SaaS Specializations

- **Subscription Models**: Freemium, tiered pricing, usage-based billing

- **Pricing Strategy**: Value-based pricing, competitive analysis, A/B testing
- **Revenue Optimization**: Upselling, cross-selling, retention strategies

### Growth & Marketing
- **Customer Acquisition**: SEO, content marketing, social media, paid advertising
- **Conversion Optimization**: Landing pages, onboarding flows, funnel analysis
- **Content Strategy**: Blog content, case studies, tutorials, documentation
- **Brand Positioning**: Market differentiation, value proposition, messaging

### Customer Success & Retention
- **Onboarding**: User activation, feature adoption, success metrics
- **Support**: Help desk, documentation, community building
- **Retention**: Engagement tracking, churn prevention, win-back campaigns
- **Feedback**: User research, feature requests, satisfaction surveys

### Technical Infrastructure
- **Scalability**: Auto-scaling, load balancing, performance monitoring
- **Analytics**: User behavior, business metrics, conversion tracking
- **Security**: Data protection, authentication, compliance (GDPR, SOC2)
- **Reliability**: Uptime monitoring, backup strategies, disaster recovery
