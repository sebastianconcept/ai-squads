# JTBD Analysis: Prelaunch Page Improvement

## Customer Jobs Analysis

### Primary Job: "When I need to automate my accounting processes, I want to find a reliable solution so that I can save time and reduce errors."

**Job Context:**
- Brazilian accountants working with multiple bank statements
- Manual data entry consuming 5+ hours per week
- High error rates in manual processing
- Pressure to deliver accurate results quickly
- Limited budget for expensive solutions

**Success Criteria:**
- Solution saves at least 3 hours per week
- Reduces data entry errors by 90%+
- Works with major Brazilian banks
- Easy to implement and use
- Affordable pricing

**Pain Points:**
- Current solutions are too complex or expensive
- Manual processes are error-prone and time-consuming
- Lack of trust in new technology
- Concerns about data security
- Uncertainty about ROI

### Emotional Job: "When I'm overwhelmed with paperwork, I want to feel confident and in control so that I can focus on strategic work."

**Emotional Context:**
- Stress from repetitive manual tasks
- Frustration with current inefficiencies
- Desire to be seen as modern and efficient
- Fear of making mistakes
- Need for professional credibility

**Emotional Success Criteria:**
- Feel more confident in work quality
- Experience reduced stress and anxiety
- Gain recognition for efficiency improvements
- Feel proud of modernizing processes

### Social Job: "When I recommend tools to colleagues, I want to be seen as knowledgeable and helpful so that I can build professional relationships."

**Social Context:**
- Professional reputation among peers
- Influence within accounting community
- Desire to help colleagues succeed
- Need to stay current with industry trends

**Social Success Criteria:**
- Colleagues seek advice and recommendations
- Recognition as early adopter of good solutions
- Enhanced professional reputation
- Stronger network relationships

## Satisfaction Gap Analysis

1. **Time-Consuming Manual Processes** (Impact: 9/10, Frequency: Daily)
   - Current: 5+ hours per week on data entry
   - Desired: <1 hour per week
   - Gap: 4+ hours weekly inefficiency

2. **Error-Prone Data Entry** (Impact: 8/10, Frequency: Weekly)
   - Current: 10-15% error rate in manual entry
   - Desired: <1% error rate
   - Gap: High risk of financial mistakes

3. **Lack of Trust in Solutions** (Impact: 7/10, Frequency: When evaluating)
   - Current: Skeptical of new tools
   - Desired: Confident in solution reliability
   - Gap: Hesitation to adopt new technology

   - Desired: Quick, intuitive setup

5. **Unclear ROI Demonstration** (Impact: 6/10, Frequency: When deciding)
   - Current: Uncertain about value proposition
   - Desired: Clear cost-benefit analysis
   - Gap: Decision paralysis

## Solution Alignment Validation

### How Our Solution Addresses Customer Jobs

**Primary Job Alignment:**
✅ **Time Savings**: Clear messaging about 5+ hours saved per week
✅ **Error Reduction**: Specific statistics about accuracy improvements
✅ **Bank Compatibility**: List of supported Brazilian banks
✅ **Ease of Use**: Simple setup and intuitive interface
✅ **Affordable Pricing**: Transparent pricing with free trial

**Emotional Job Alignment:**
✅ **Confidence Building**: Success stories and testimonials
✅ **Stress Reduction**: Focus on automation benefits
✅ **Professional Credibility**: Modern, professional design
✅ **Control**: Clear process and predictable results

**Social Job Alignment:**
✅ **Peer Recommendations**: Referral program and testimonials
✅ **Industry Leadership**: Early adopter positioning
✅ **Knowledge Sharing**: Educational content and case studies
✅ **Community Building**: User success stories

### Unintended Consequences Analysis

**Potential Negative Outcomes:**
1. **Over-Promise**: Setting unrealistic expectations about automation
   - Mitigation: Use conservative estimates and real user data
   
2. **Security Concerns**: Highlighting automation may raise data security fears
   - Mitigation: Emphasize security certifications and compliance
   
3. **Job Displacement Anxiety**: Fear that automation reduces job value
   - Mitigation: Position as tool that enhances professional skills

4. **Complexity Perception**: Too many features may seem overwhelming
   - Mitigation: Focus on core value proposition and simple messaging
