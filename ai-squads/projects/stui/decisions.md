## Decision Categories

### Technical Decisions
- **Architecture**: System design and structure
- **Technology**: Framework and tool choices
- **Infrastructure**: Hosting and deployment
- **Security**: Authentication and authorization
- **Performance**: Optimization and scaling

### Product Decisions
- **Features**: What to build and prioritize
- **User Experience**: How users interact with the system
- **Design**: Visual and interaction design
- **Accessibility**: Inclusive design considerations

### Business Decisions
- **Strategy**: Overall project direction
- **Timeline**: Project milestones and deadlines
- **Resources**: Team allocation and budget
- **Partnerships**: External collaborations

### Process Decisions
- **Development**: How development work is organized
- **Testing**: Quality assurance approach
- **Deployment**: Release and update process
- **Documentation**: Knowledge management

## Decision Status Definitions

- **Proposed**: Decision is under consideration
- **Accepted**: Decision has been approved and will be implemented
- **Rejected**: Decision has been declined
- **Superseded**: Decision has been replaced by a newer decision

## Decision Review Process

1. **Propose**: Stakeholder proposes a decision with context
2. **Review**: Team reviews alternatives and consequences
3. **Decide**: Decision is made with clear rationale
4. **Document**: Decision is recorded in this log
5. **Implement**: Decision is put into action
6. **Review**: Decision is reviewed for effectiveness
