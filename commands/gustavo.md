## Financial Frameworks Reference

### Transaction Categorization Framework
```
Primary Categories:
- Income: Salary, business revenue, investments, other
- Expense: Operating, administrative, capital, personal
- Transfer: Between accounts, loans, repayments
- Investment: Purchases, sales, dividends, interest

Tax Categories:
- Deductible: Business expenses, certain personal deductions
- Non-deductible: Personal expenses, penalties
- Taxable: Investment gains, certain income types

Business Categories:
- COGS: Cost of goods sold
- Operating: Day-to-day business expenses
- Administrative: Overhead and management costs
- Capital: Long-term investments and assets
```

### Cost Optimization Process
```
1. Identify: List all expenses and their purposes
2. Analyze: Necessity, frequency, alternatives, ROI
3. Prioritize: High impact, low effort first
4. Negotiate: Better terms, discounts, alternatives
5. Eliminate: Remove unnecessary or low-value expenses
6. Monitor: Track savings and prevent expense creep
7. Optimize: Continuous improvement cycle
```

### Revenue Expansion Framework
```
1. Current State: Analyze existing revenue streams
2. Opportunities: Identify expansion possibilities
3. Pricing: Optimize pricing strategies (value-based, tiered)
4. Volume: Increase transaction frequency/quantity
5. Value: Enhance per-transaction value (upselling)
6. Channels: Explore new revenue channels
7. Markets: Consider geographic or demographic expansion
8. Products: Develop complementary offerings
```

### Wealth Building Strategy
```
Foundation (Months 1-6):
- Build emergency fund (6-12 months expenses)
- Eliminate high-interest debt
- Establish savings habit (20-30% of income)

Growth (Months 7-24):
- Maximize tax-advantaged accounts
- Build diversified investment portfolio
- Increase income streams
- Optimize tax situation

Acceleration (Year 3+):
- Compound wealth through investments
- Develop passive income streams
- Plan for financial independence
- Consider estate planning
```

## Brazilian Tax Context

### Personal Tax (IRPF)
- **Tax Brackets**: Progressive rates from 0% to 22.5%
- **Deductions**: Health, education, dependents, donations
- **Tax-Advantaged Investments**: LCI, LCA (tax-exempt)
- **Capital Gains**: 15% on stocks, 15-22.5% on other assets
- **Annual Declaration**: Required above income threshold

### Business Tax Regimes
- **Simples Nacional**: Simplified tax for small businesses
- **Presumido**: Presumed profit method
- **Real**: Actual profit method (full accounting)
- **MEI**: Individual micro-entrepreneur (simplified)

### Key Tax Concepts
- **Dedutível**: Expenses that reduce taxable income
- **Isento**: Tax-exempt income or investments
- **Retido na Fonte**: Tax withheld at source
- **Declaração Anual**: Annual tax return
- **CPF/CNPJ**: Individual/company tax ID
