## Key Copywriting Frameworks

### AIDA (Atenção, Interesse, Desejo, Ação)
```
Atenção: Headline que para o scroll
Interesse: Mostre relevância para o contexto brasileiro
Desejo: Crie conexão emocional e aspiracional
Ação: CTA claro, específico, com urgência genuína
```

### PAS (Problema, Agitação, Solução)
```
Problema: Identifique dor específica do mercado brasileiro
Agitação: Amplifique o custo emocional e financeiro
Solução: Apresente seu SaaS como a resposta ideal
```

### 4 P's (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push)
```
Promise: Promessa clara de transformação
Picture: Pinte o futuro melhor com seu produto
Proof: Prova social de clientes brasileiros
Push: Call-to-action com urgência ética
```

### Before-After-Bridge
```
Before: Situação atual (dor, frustração)
After: Situação ideal (transformação, sucesso)
Bridge: Seu SaaS como a ponte entre os dois estados
```

### Feature-Advantage-Benefit (FAB)
```
Feature: O que é (funcionalidade técnica)
Advantage: Como funciona (aplicação prática)
Benefit: Por que importa (impacto no negócio brasileiro)
```

## Brazilian Market Principles

### Cultural Adaptation
- **Warmth & Relationship**: Brazilians value personal connection (quente vs. frio)
- **Storytelling**: Use narrative, emotion, and human stories
- **Informality**: Balance professionalism with approachability (você vs. senhor)
- **Optimism**: Embrace Brazilian positivity and solution-focus
- **Community**: Emphasize belonging and social connection
- **Trust Building**: Requires more touchpoints than US/EU markets
- **Social Proof**: Essential - Brazilians look to peers for validation

### Brazilian Business Context
- **Longer Sales Cycles**: More relationship building needed
- **Price Sensitivity**: Address ROI and value explicitly
- **Risk Aversion**: Provide guarantees, trials, and security signals
- **Hierarchical**: Respect organizational structures in messaging
- **WhatsApp First**: Primary business communication channel
- **Payment Methods**: Consider Boleto, PIX, installments (parcelamento)
- **Tax Complexity**: Be transparent about pricing

### Language & Tone Guidelines

**Brazilian Portuguese Best Practices**
- Use "você" for most SaaS contexts (not "tu" or "vós")
- Avoid European Portuguese expressions
- Incorporate Brazilian idioms naturally
- Balance formality: professional but warm
- Use active voice and direct language
- Avoid anglicisms when Portuguese terms exist
- Test copy with native Brazilian speakers

**Tone Variations by Audience**
- **Startups/Tech**: Informal, innovative, energetic
- **SMBs**: Practical, ROI-focused, supportive
- **Enterprise**: Professional, strategic, consultative
- **Founders**: Empathetic, aspirational, community-oriented

## Ethical Guidelines

### ✅ Ethical Copywriting Practices
- Tell the truth about product capabilities
- Use genuine scarcity and urgency (not fake timers)
- Show real testimonials from real customers
- Transparent about pricing and terms
- Respect user intelligence and decision-making
- Provide clear value before asking for conversion
- Support claims with evidence
- Make cancellation and refunds clear
- Address objections honestly

### ❌ Avoid Dark Patterns
- Fake countdown timers
- False scarcity claims
- Misleading comparisons
- Hidden fees or conditions
- Confirmshaming language
- Fabricated social proof
- Bait-and-switch offers
- Forced continuity without clear notice
